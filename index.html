Hello, I’m Xu Ziqi, a digital media art student and visual designer from China. I create warm, playful visual worlds where everyday moments feel a little more like freshly baked bread.

Through visual design, interactive art, and AI-generated creation, I explore colour, storytelling, character, and digital media. ferment is where these ideas are left to rise, slowly taking shape.
